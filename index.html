Welcome!

Welcome to the Academy of Baseball, a club organization based in the San Diego North County area and run by high school baseball coaches. We are dedicated to developing young athletes with a focus on high school and college preparation, and are excited to offer competitive teams with limited rosters for players aged 13U through 18U.

At the Academy of Baseball, our mission is to foster team cohesion as a key differentiator from other club programs, where there is often a lot of family and player movement from season to season. We aim for our players to stay together as they progress through high school, believing that consistency and camaraderie are essential for developing not only skilled athletes but also lifelong friendships.

In alignment with 5 Tool Hitting Center and the KORE Performance facility (inside 5 Tool San Marcos), we focus on training baseball-relevant athleticism, providing our players with the tools they need to excel both on and off the field. Our year-round program guarantees consistent club play, allowing athletes to hone their skills and improve their game throughout the entire year.

Program Details and Costs

We offer 2 packages, which vary in cost and commitment. Note that each age group is intended to be a continuous cohort of approximately 30-40 players. The packages are not designed to be a "tryout" based hierarchy, but rather a fluid system that allows for players to move in and out of more time-intensive squads in alignment with their schedule and interest. Note that, depending on enrollment numbers within each age group, not all packages may be available across all seasons. When this is the case, we work with the families and players to determine the optimal plan and price structure that meets their needs.

Field + Batting + 1 KORE Workout

This package includes 1 field practice and 1 batting cage practice per week at the 5 Tool facility, plus 1 weekly workout at the KORE Performance facility. KORE workouts target baseball-aligned athleticism and strength training, serving as a key accelerator to overall athlete development. Scrimmages and tournaments are available to these players, with any associated costs communicated in advance when applicable.

Field + Batting + 2 KORE Workouts

This is the most demanding package, with the same field practice and batting cage session as above, plus 2 KORE Performance facility workouts per week. Also included is a monthly membership to the 5 Tool Hitting Center, which allows your player to reserve up to 1/2 hour of tunnel time per day. Players at this level get priority to RSVP for scrimmage and tournament rosters.
