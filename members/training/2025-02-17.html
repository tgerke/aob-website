Welcome to this week's defense training tip and drill! This week we're focusing on proper lower body mechanics for fielding ground balls. You've heard me talk a lot lately about the difference between a squat where your weight is on your heels (which we don't want) versus more of a hinge where your butt is pushed back and your glove is out front (yes!). This video does a great job showing this, and the drill is a great thing you can do around the house to build some muscle memory during all the rain this week.

Featured Video

Key Points:

  • Make sure you check your posture in each position of the walking drill
  • Don't let your feet get too close together, especially in the normal middle position; keep a good wide base
  • Don't do the drill by falling back through your heels; stay on the balls of your feet, emphasizing your glove forward and physically ready to make a forward throw
