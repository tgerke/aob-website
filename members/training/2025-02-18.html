Today we're focusing on our two recent favorite hitting drills: one-handed swings and hook 'em. Review the videos in depth to see how these drill should look and, in the example of the hook 'em video, some examples of what NOT to do.

One-handed swings

Key Points from Video

  • Notice where he finishes his swing (not low after a rollover!)
  • Posture and barrel control strength is key
  • The "stop" drill at the end is a bonus, and one we also do in our hitting sessions

Hook 'em

Key Points from Video

  • The mistake examples are awesome, because I see variations of all of them in our cage work
  • Watch how he strides in a straight line forward, and his shoulders have good linear direction straight at the pitcher (not up or down)

Remember: Quality over quantity. Focus on doing each rep correctly rather than rushing through the drills. Questions? Let's talk at practice!
